About Our Life: Beginnings & Always

Our Life: Beginnings & Always is an immersive visual novel developed by GB Patch Games that takes you through four life stages - childhood, adolescence, teenage years, and young adulthood. Set in the charming coastal town of Sunset Bird, you'll grow alongside your childhood friend and potential love interest, Derek Flores.

What makes this visual novel special is its deep customization options. You can change your character's appearance, personality traits, and interests at the beginning of each life stage, reflecting how people naturally evolve over time.

Key Game Elements:

  • Inclusive character customization with options for pronouns, appearance, and personality traits
  • Branching storylines based on your choices and relationship development
  • LGBTQ+ friendly narrative with romance options
  • Beautiful artwork and engaging soundtrack
  • Heartwarming coming-of-age story that explores friendship, family, and personal growth

Community Recognition

"Our Life: Beginnings & Always" has received widespread acclaim for its heartfelt storytelling, inclusive representation, and player-centric design, making it one of the most beloved visual novels in recent years.
